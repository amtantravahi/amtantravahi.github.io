    ABOUT ME

    Born in Ghana, raised in America and India living in Austin. I am currently an Economics and Government student at The University of Texas at Austin. I work as an RA and coteam leader of the Data4Peace team at the Innovations for Peace and Development lab at UT Austin. My work includes corruption, game theory, civil war territorial control, and methodology. Mainly my interests lie in politics, development, data science, and sports analytics.

    Below are my skills and I'm open to working with NGOs, non-profits, and researchers for data analysis/visualizations!
